2018 Conference

Conference 2018 was a great success, it had more than 350 participants across Canada.

Conference 2018 is a historical event. It is the very first time a nationwide conference about AI specifically for high school students in Canada. it raises more awareness among youth about AI and provides great hands on learning opportunities. It also creates a rare opportunity for universities, industrial leaders, educational organizations, and cities to support our next generation.
