Our Program

We are proud to offere the following to help increase AI awareness among youth:

National Conference

Inspire students by organizing guest speaker sessions and demonstrations from regional and international research institutions and businesses to discuss AI work or product.

Empowerment Program

  • Internship Programs
  • AI related course learning
  • Experiential learning
  • Help on student-driven AI innovation projects by collaborating resources needed

AI professors and experts demystify AI related questions from students, to raise awareness of AI development and inform AI opportunities.

Advocacy

Petition and document support to integrate AI courses into provincial education curriculums for high schools students across Canada.
